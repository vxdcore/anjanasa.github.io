About Me

I'm an Industrial & Accessory Designer, graduated from the National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Mumbai. My practice spans fashion accessories, leather goods, and lifestyle products — bridging cultural craft traditions with contemporary design sensibility.

From collaborating directly with artisans at the Kolhapuri Chappal Craft Cluster to leading art direction at a premium lifestyle brand, I bring both cultural sensitivity and commercial pragmatism to every project.

Skilled in CMF, concept development, sketching, and prototyping — I design from first idea to finished object, balancing aesthetic vision with commercial viability.
